Welcome to The Recollector

An AI-Powered Memory Companion

Our mission: We’re a team of engineers building technology that helps people stay connected to what matters most — their memories. For individuals living with Alzheimer’s and other cognitive challenges, remembering a password can be stressful. So we asked: what if your memories could be the key?

The Recollector transforms authentication into a gentle, memory-reinforcing experience. Instead of typing a password, users simply recognize familiar photos — moments, faces, places — to securely log in.

Just upload 5 meaningful images. These become your personal “memory key.” When you log in, you'll complete a series of short visual challenges to identify them among lookalikes. Each round is a moment of reconnection — not just security.

It’s not just for security. It’s for preserving the past, supporting the present, and strengthening memory through familiarity and repetition — with the help of AI.
